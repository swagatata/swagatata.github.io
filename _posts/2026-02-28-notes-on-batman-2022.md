The first time I watched Batman, I couldn't finish it. It drowsed me into stupor twice before I could get halfway. Not because this was a boring film, but the only way my insomniac ass can fall asleep is when I'm thoroughly out of my brain. 

But then after months, I remembered the movie again and watched it. 

How can a movie make me feel this way? 

The Nolan movies were amazing(not you, The Dark Knight Rises, not you!) in a way and made me celebrate Batman as a hero. But this, this felt like Batman is my guy. The movie didn't say it's better than me, it made me relax, assured me I'm ok as I am and brought the Batman to me.
